01 / Overview

Mission Brief

Our Capstone project delivers a fixed-wing VTOL UAV purpose-built for search and rescue operations. The aircraft combines the hover precision of a multirotor with the endurance and range of a fixed-wing platform, enabling deployment into challenging terrain without a runway.
